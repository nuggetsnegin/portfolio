About

Hi there, I'm Negin [nə'giːn] and I'm a front-end developer based in Toronto, Canada! I got my start in web-development back in the late 90s when I was styling my amazing Neopets profile page and creating Sailor Moon fanpages in Geocities. I'm passionate about creating beautiful, responsive and accessible websites.

I have a Bachelor of Applied Computer science from Dalhousie University and a Certificate of Web Development Immersive from Juno.

Oh, and I have 3 cats.

Let's work together.
